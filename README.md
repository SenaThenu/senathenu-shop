## Getting Started 🌱

Simple! Just fork this repository and fill in the information in products.json. You can have any number of products.

> \[!NOTE]
> The key of each product will be dynamically used as the URI. Just replace the sample product names in `images_location`, `preview_img` and `hover_preview_img` with your product name!
